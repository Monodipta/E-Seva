# E-Seva

**E-Seva** is an Android application designed to simplify communication between citizens and municipal authorities. It serves as a centralized platform to access municipal services, register complaints, and stay informed about initiatives and opportunities.

---

## Features

### 1. Scheme Information
Stay informed about various municipal schemes, their benefits, and eligibility criteria.

### 2. Complaint Registration and Tracking
- Register complaints for issues like water, electricity, sanitation, and more.
- Track the status of your complaints in real time.

### 3. Recruitment Updates
Access job postings and recruitment drives conducted by the municipal corporation.

### 4. Women’s Services
Dedicated resources for women’s welfare, including information on government initiatives and support systems.

### 5. Know Your Corporator
Find details about your local corporator to foster better community engagement.

---

## Technical Overview

- **Frontend**: Built with **Java** for Android, offering a responsive and intuitive user interface.
- **Backend**: Powered by **Firebase**, ensuring real-time data management and seamless performance.

---

## How to Use

1. **Download** the E-Seva app on your Android device.
2. **Register** with your mobile number and details.
3. Navigate through sections like:
   - **Schemes** for government programs.
   - **Complaints** to register and track issues.
   - **Recruitment** for job-related information.
   - **Women’s Services** for specialized resources.
4. **Stay Updated**: Receive notifications on updates and changes.
